MOT Troubleshooting

Lots of scattered light ~100 mV on PMT (remember pmt out is negative)

No repump because not talking to SynthHD -> That's a problem (fixed now but not connecting to SynthHD is a problem to be aware of. To solve just restart the computer, if no windows update)

On camera see fluorescence from beam but need to position camera correctly for focus (4f imaging system)

Dispenser sitting at 6.5 A for a couple of hours. Unclear when it had "turned on enough" for good signal.

To optimize signal on PMT: turn down aom to 21 dbm (you can keep frequency at 89 dbm), increase area of MOT beam, turn up gain on PMT.

To get camera in approximately correct position: decrease size of mot beam, look for beam on live image preview.

Binning camera, ~300 ms exposure.
