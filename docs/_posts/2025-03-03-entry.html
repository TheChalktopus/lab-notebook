Fiber EOM Testing

\[16 dbm = 10*Log10[Vpp^2/2.82^2/50] + 30\] \[Vpp \approx 4 \text{V}\] Using, \(\Beta = V_\text{pp}/(2 V_\pi)\), we find \(\Beta \approx 0.42\), which conveniently gives a ratio peaks of \[BesselJ[1,beta]^2/(BesselJ[0, beta]^2 + 2*BesselJ[1, beta]^2) = 0.042\]

On the scope we actually find a ratio of \(0.026\).

Repeating this calculation for 14 dBm, we find \(\Beta = 0.34\), which gives a ratio \[BesselJ[1,beta]^2/(BesselJ[0, beta]^2 + 2*BesselJ[1, beta]^2) = 0.028\]
