MFD of fibers on EOM and our PM 630 fibers different. So let's try with PM 850 Fibers (from Newport)

Find correct fiber collimator see selection guide on thorlabs website https://www.thorlabs.com/newgrouppage9.cfm?objectgroup_id=2940

\[MFD = \varnothing_\text{spot} = \frac{4 \lambda f }{\pi D} \Rightarrow f = \frac{\pi D \text{MFD}}{4 \lambda}\]

Sample calculation for PM 630 fibers we're using already for Rb MOT. MFD = 4.5 $μ$m @ 630 nm. \[f = \frac{\pi (0.001 \text{ m})(4.5 \times 10^{-6} \text{ m})}{4(780 \times 10^{-9})} = 0.00453 \text{ m}\] which lines up with \(f = 4.51 mm\) that we are using.

Calculation for PM 850 fibers we'll need for Rb fiber EOM. MFD = 5.5 $μ$m @ 850 nm. \[f = \frac{\pi (0.001 \text{ m})(5.5 \times 10^{-6} \text{ m})}{4(780 \times 10^{-9})} = 0.00553 \text{ m}\]
